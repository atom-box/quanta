What is Zumtum?

Start by taking something you would like to memorize, like notes, or an article. Zunthum will guide you in turning it into a series of questions that summarize the main points. It will then ask you to synthesize a paragraph that answers your series of questions. If you sign in with an email address you will be given a chance to save your notes.

Born in the Ivy League

This method of studying is called 'Cornell Notes' since it was promoted by a professor at Cornell College going all the way back to the 1940s.

Used by AVID

Today this method of studying is promoted by AVID Schools, a network of over 700 public & private schools based in Sandy Ego, California. FontAwesome icons available.

Make it yours

This technique is not an orthodoxy but rather just a trick to get you to view a set of ideas in a layered, structured, timed way. FontAwesome icons available.

Study smarter

By giving you a simple set of steps it helps you relax and enjoy learning.
